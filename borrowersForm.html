ENGINEERING LABORATORY

BORROWING PROCEDURE

1.THE PROFESSOR MUST INSTRUCT THE LEADERS OF THE EXPERIMENT TO SECURE AND SUBMIT BORROWER'S SLIP FROM THE LABORATORY DISPENSING UNIT AT LEAST 4 DAYS BEFORE THE SCHEDULED LABORATORY CLASS.

2.EACH GROUP MUST PERFORM THE EXPERIMENT ON THE TABLE DESIGNATED BY THE PROFESSOR OF THE CLASS.

3.SHARING OF APPARATUS/EQUIPMENT/MATERIALS IS NOT ALLOWED.

4.AFTER THE EXPERIMENT, THE LEADER MUST RETURN THE APPARATUS /EQUIPMENT AND MUST ENSURE TO CLEAN THE TABLE BEFORE LEAVING.

5.ALL MEMBERS OF THE GROUP SHALL BE HELD ACCOUNTABLE FOR ANY DAMAGE OR LOSS OF THE BORROWED APPARATUS/EQUIPMENT DUE NEGLIGENCE OR CARELESSNESS.

6.AFTER THE EXPERIMENT, ALL BORROWED APPARATUS/EQUIPMENT SHOULD BE RETURNED 10 MINUTES BEFORE THE END OF THE CLASS.
